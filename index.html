Features

Everything we offer to you for your great Homestay Host experience

Continuous Placing

Thankfully, Vancouver is one of the best country for study in Japan so we're sure students are going to come next couple of years. That allows us to offer student to you continuously.

Flexible Host Schedule

Our students normally stay around 1~2 months so you can manage your rooms however you want. When your kids is back to your house, you just take a break for Homestay. Easy.

Troubleless Homestay

Our 99% students are coming from Japan. We are proud of the Japanese politeness. We believe that helps your homestay a lot.

Homestay Friendly

Our Agency help your Homestay Journey. We communicate our student when something happen during homestay. Also, you can take a break if you are tired of homestay.

Process

Every Processes are very simple and easy

01
Tell us your basic Info

First of all, we need your basic information such as your name, email-address, and phone number (optional).
Please fill up our apply form in this website and submit it.

02
Fill up and Send the applications back to us

Once you get our applications for Homestay Host family, read our homestay rules and details. If you agree with them, please fill in your detail information and send them back to us.

03
Inspection and Place the student

After we receive your application, we will visit you an inspection. If you pass the inspection, you are finally a new homestay host family.
We will place the student to your house!
